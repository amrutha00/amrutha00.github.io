TTF-ST: Diversified Text to Face Generation using Search and Transform

Springer Link

Python, tensorflow, BERT, MobileNetV2, StyleGAN2

  • Developed a deep learning pipeline to synthesize realistic human face images from natural language text descriptions
  • Ideated and implemented an algorithm to augment the CelebA dataset with grammatically accurate captions from 40 facial attributes
  • Employed probabilistic sampling to handle dataset imbalance of labels in the dataset, leading to a 5% increase in overall accuracy
  • Fine-tuned BERT to extract text embedding from textual descriptions with a remarkable AUC of 0.999 and Sigmoid loss of 0.008
  • Outperformed SOTA models by 47%, leveraging a novel search & transform approach with StyleGAN2 to boost image fidelity
